ICT 9 PORTFOLIO

What is ICT 9 Portfolio?

This is a portfolio composed of the different School Year Events and Activities, Coding lessons, Quarter Reflections, and All About Me. This is an overall compilation of everything that occurred this school year in ICT 9.





School Events and Activities

The school events and activities page show a compilation of educational events, programs, and activities that occurred during a certain school quarter. They can be group works, competitions, awareness and learning programs, individual activities, and the like.



Quarter Lessons

The quarter lessons page show the topics discussed during a specific quarter. It presents an application of the lessons, showing both the input and output of the examples.



All About Me

The All About Me page presents details about the website developer. The page shows the coder's name, age, school, interests, and hobbies. It also shows some of his recommendations to the website users.



Reflection

The reflection page presents the thoughts of the student about the lessons discussed per quarter. It shows what the student learned, how he understands the lessons, and practical applications of the lessons.
